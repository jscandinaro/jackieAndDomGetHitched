Dom Scandinaro

Dom is a software engineer from Pittsburgh, PA. He is a die hard Steelers, Penguins, and Pirates fan and constantly reminds Jackie how many super bowls the Steelers have won. Shortly after Jackie moved to Chicago Dom was golfing at the same course he did all summer when he noticed a beautiful new bartender. When she was taking care of him and a couple of his friends, he asked her to the Rainforest Cafe for a date as part of a bet. Dom told Jackie that he had a special reserved table near the monkeys and that he was a regular. Jackie was very intrigued and decided to say yes. About 30 minutes after their first date ended Dom asked Jackie out on a second date, and the rest was history!

Proposal

On November 7th, 2015 Dom took Jackie for a walk to the bean after work. Once they arrived he put headphones on her and a special mix of some of their favorite songs began playing. After listening to music for a few minutes while staring at the bean Dom got down on one knee and popped the question. Many spectators began yelling and chearing in excitement, and before Dom could even finish asking the question Jackie was already saying YES! They then spent a private weekend together while only sharing the news with their close friends and family before making it public knowledge the following week.
